# Hi! I'm Alicia JW Takaoka

### AI, Software Engineering, and Systems Researcher

Intersectional data and design science educator. Technology,
transitions, and inclusion.

I am a Postdoctoral Researcher at Rotterdam School of Management,
Erasmus University Rotterdam, where I explore how AI, data, and
digitalization can support more just and equitable futures.

My work brings together:

- Intersectional Software Engineering
- Energy Justice
- Ethical AI
- Human-Centered Design
- Data Literacy
- Participatory Innovation

My mission is to create inclusive spaces that improve diversity in
the digital landscape.

My vision is to prioritize AI ethics and inclusive human-computer
interaction by amplifying marginalized voices, Indigenous knowledge,
and the experiences of women through participatory design,
data-driven decision making, and data literacy.

This work supports collaboration among communities, governments,
universities, and industry working toward more sustainable,
equitable, and responsible futures.
